Who We Are

TravelSphere is a travel planner based in Lahore, crafting authentic local and international experiences. We believe that travel is not just about visiting places, but about immersing yourself in cultures, making connections, and creating unforgettable memories.

From cultural city tours to remote nature retreats, we design trips that turn moments into stories. Whether you're seeking adventure in Pakistan's northern valleys or exploring the romantic streets of Paris, we ensure every detail is personalized for you.

Meet Our Team

Mehak Iqbal

Founder & CEO

Mehak's love for travel began at the age of 18, when she backpacked across the northern areas of Pakistan. With over a decade of experience in the tourism industry, she founded TravelSphere to help others discover the same joy in exploring the world. She specializes in curating premium travel packages blending comfort, authenticity, and adventure.
